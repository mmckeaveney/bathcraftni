ABOUT US

Some more information about who we are and what we do.

Our services and what we do

Bathcraft NI is a bathroom refurbishment specialist based in the Greater Belfast Area, Northern Ireland. We cover all six counties in Northern Ireland and we are willing to travel to please our customers. As well as complete bathroom refurbishment we also offer a full plumbing and tiling service.

Plumbing

Plumbing services we provide:

  • Installation & maintainance of unvented & mains pressure systems
  • Tap replacement in bathroom and kichen
  • New water mains installed
  • Leaking pipes
  • Cold water storage tanks
  • Immersion heaters
  • General plumbing repairs

Tiling

We provide a full wall to floor tiling service. Choose from a wide range of creative tiling designs and materials such as:

  • Porcelain
  • Ceramic
  • Natural Stone
  • Granite
  • Slate
  • Marble
  • Glass
  • Mosaic

Electrical

Electrical services we provide include -

  • Electric Showers
  • Thermostatic showers
  • Audio Sound Systems
  • Mood lighting
  • Motion Sensored Switching (Lighting, Audio, Showers and more.)
  • Extractor Fans
